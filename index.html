Welcome

Welcome to Playful Pathways Therapy, a new pediatric occupational therapy practice opening fall 2023 in Crosby, MN.

The Playful Pathways name came from the fact that individuals continue to form new neural pathways in the brain in response to experiences. And in children these experiences are often through play! These pathways are how children learn new skills or relearn past skills lost through an injury or illness.

At Playful Pathways, we will work together as a team (therapist, patient, and family) to help your child develop skills and abilities in a playful environment.

Playful Pathways Therapy will be located in Cuyuna Lakes Community Church (formally Crosby Assembly of God).

Occupational Therapists look at each individual as a whole. We use a combination of techniques to work on different skills. For children, we use play to break down an activity’s tasks and make learning fun. Some areas occupational therapists can work on include:

  • Fine Motor Skills
  • Gross Motor Skills
  • Sensory Integration
  • Emotional Regulation
  • Activities of Daily Living (Feeding, Dressing, Self Cares)

Physician referral is required to receive occupational therapy services. Please let me know if you need assistance with this.
